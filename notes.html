Calculus – Derivatives

May 14, 2024

Key Rules:

  • Power Rule: d/dx (xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹
  • Product Rule: d/dx [u·v] = u·dv/dx + v·du/dx
  • Chain Rule: d/dx [f(g(x))] = f'(g(x)) · g'(x)

Examples:

  • d/dx (x³) = 3x²
  • d/dx (sin x) = cos x
  • d/dx (eˣ) = eˣ
